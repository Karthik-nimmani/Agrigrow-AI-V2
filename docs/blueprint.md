# **App Name**: AgriGrow AI

## Core Features:

- Secure OTP Authentication: Secure login via Phone (OTP) or Google, optimized for rural ease of use.
- Farm Field Management: Full CRUD operations for tracking crop types, soil pH, area, and yield history.
- AI Yield Forecasts: Predictive modeling using Gemini 1.5 Pro/Flash to estimate upcoming harvests based on field data.
- Explainable AI Insights: Transparent logic describing why specific yields are predicted, such as rainfall or fertilizer impact.
- Generative AI Recommendations: A tool that provides actionable advice (e.g., 'Apply 50kg lime/acre') dynamically based on soil chemistry and yield forecasts.
- Interactive Dashboard: A personal analytics hub for farmers to visualize historical tracking and current farm data.
- Offline-First Resilience: Client-side caching to enable viewing and limited interaction with field data without an active internet connection.
- Live Meteorological Intelligence: Real-time synchronization of local temperature and humidity via OpenWeather Integration, providing dynamic alerts like frost warnings and irrigation timing based on atmospheric conditions.
- AI Farm Assistant (Agri-Bot): A persistent, multi-language chat assistant tool that answers real-time agricultural queries.
- Professional Analytics: High-impact bar and line charts (Recharts Visualization) comparing predicted vs. actual yields and seasonal growth trajectories.
- AI Vision Suite: A tool that uses computer vision for Florafix Disease Detection (identifying pests/diseases from photos and providing a 3-part treatment plan) and Soil Report Scanning (extracting metrics like pH, NPK, Moisture from physical lab reports).

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A grounded, rich golden-brown (#A36B27) symbolizing harvest and fertile earth, providing stable contrast for a light theme.
- Background color: A subtle, desaturated off-white (#F7F3ED) offering a clean and calm canvas, visibly derived from the primary hue for harmony with natural settings.
- Accent color: A soft, warm rosy tan (#D6B6AC) for analogous yet distinct contrast, adding warmth and visual interest to interactive elements and highlights.
- Primary font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for both headlines and body text, ensuring modern readability and clarity across all data and recommendations.
- Utilize Lucide Icons with clear, concise, and agricultural-themed representations to ensure immediate understanding of actions and data points.
- Employ a mobile-first, responsive design with clear grid-based layouts to present farm data and AI insights effectively on small screens.
- Incorporate subtle and functional animations for smooth transitions when navigating, updating data, or displaying AI generated information, enhancing user experience without distraction.
